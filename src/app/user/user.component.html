Welcome to Lancs-LAMP Testing System

This is a digital solution that has been designed collaboratively with local NHS organisations as a secure and effective way to share images and results across the Lancashire and South Cumbria Integrated Care System (ICS).

The service is an application that patients and staff members can use to carry out tests, support the process of providing samples and submit any related information or images, in a variety of settings and for many different uses e.g. COVID-19 tests. Updates and sesults are then sent back to the user of the app to enable them to view the outcome of each test.
